# Federal Reserve COVID Report

### Analyzing Federal Reserve Policy Effectiveness During COVID-Era Inflation

---

## Overview

This research project evaluates the effectiveness of Federal Reserve monetary policy during the COVID-19 pandemic and subsequent inflationary period. Through statistical analysis and economic modeling, we examine how Fed interventions impacted inflation rates, employment levels, and overall economic recovery during one of the most volatile periods in recent economic history. The complete research findings are available in `Federal Reserve Policy Effectiveness Report.pdf`

## Key Research Areas

### Monetary Policy Analysis
Examination of interest rate decisions, quantitative easing programs, and emergency lending facilities

### Inflation Dynamics
Statistical analysis of inflation trends, supply chain disruptions, and price stability measures

### Employment Impact
Assessment of labor market recovery and the Fed's dual mandate performance

### Policy Effectiveness
Evaluation of policy tools and their real-world economic outcomes during crisis conditions
